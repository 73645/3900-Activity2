About us

We are the worlds best University

The University of Nebraska Omaha (UNO) known as a public research university in Omaha, Nebraska. The University of Nebraska Omaha is an impactful and dynamic institution deeply rooted in its community, dedicated to excellence in both research and education. It offers flexibility to students through online classes. UNO extends a warm welcome to its students, organizing events like Durango Days for newcomers. The university fosters a friendly environment and places a strong focus on IT and CS technology. UNO's campus in Omaha boasts modern facilities and state-of-the-art laboratories. The institution actively promotes community engagement and service, allowing students to apply their knowledge to real-world situations. UNO provides diverse academic programs in various fields and is known by its commitment to community engagement. UNO has a strong emphasis on inclusion and diversity, UNO encourages a welcoming atmosphere for its faculty and students. The campus has modern facilities and encourages firsthand learning experiences. The university's impact extends beyond academics, contributing to Omaha's economic and cultural landscape through partnerships with local businesses and organizations. UNO's online offerings provide flexibility for students, and its commitment to innovation positions graduates for success in a rapidly changing world.
